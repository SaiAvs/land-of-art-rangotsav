ABOUT RANGOTSAV

Rangotsav is the 2-days flagship annual event of The Land of Art, designed to celebrate the boundless creativity, vibrant colors, and artistic spirit of our college community. This festival brings together passionate artists, crafters, and creative minds to showcase their talents through painting, sketching, craft-making, and live art demonstrations
